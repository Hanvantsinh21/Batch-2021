Admission Notice

The school is a residential instituition for boys/girls. Admission are made once a year in April, in classes VI and IX only. Candidates must quality in the entrance examination to be eligible for admission. Boys who are between the age of 10-12 years and 13-15 years respectively on 1st July of the year in which admisiion is sought are eligible to take the examination for entry in Class VI and IX respectively. Subject to availability of sufficient Candidates for the writtern examination, centres will be Almora, Dehradun, Ghorakhal and Haldwani. Allotment of examination centre will be at the descreation of the school. The Candidates of Uttarakhand domicile will be entitled for 67% seats including reservation for SC/ST/ Defence categories and 33% seats are offfered to boys of other states. The entrance examination for selection of Candidates is normally held on the first sunday in January, every year.

Admission Procedure

Please do online registration on official website. Fill the form online, download the form and submit in the office with relevant documents before the last date. verification of documents by school and interaction with the candidate final list of candidate will be declared on our website.

Rules & Regulations



  Only online mode of payment of school fees will be accepted in the state bank of india through SBI debit card (ATM) if there is no SBI account the others back user can pay through internet banking system.

  School fees will be accepted online through State Bank of India (SB Collect) from 1st to 15th day only of April, July October and January month on quarterly basis. As per following progress:- (www.onlinesbi.com > SB Collect >Classic Version > then Proceed > Select State- Bihar > Select Type –Education Institution >Go > Select Education Institutions > Happy Valley School and Submit then Select Category >School Fees > after providing details at the time of online payment click click on submit button according to SB collect online systems. Please read the instructions and guidelines carefully visiting the site (www.onlinesbi.com before final submission of online payment.

  Bank charges if applicable as per bank norms will be collected.

  After 15th of every a moth fine of Rs. 100/- per month will be charged for late payment of tuition fee and transport fee separately. If the fee remains unpaid till 15th day of the next month, the name of the student will automatically be struck off the rolls and Re-admission charge of Rs. 500/- will have to be paid.

  All outstanding dues will be payable along with the fees for the month of February & will March. It must be paid by the 15th of January itself, failing which the student will not be allowed to appear in the Examination.

  Those seeking admission in the mid-session shall have to pay the fees from the beginning of the session itself.

  Advance payment of fees will be accepted.

  The school reserves the right to amend policies, rules and regulations without prior notice.


Admission to Hostel


Words are not enough to describe the DPS Vasant Kunj hostel, a three storyed brick structure housing 36 boys and 36 girls. It is imbued with warmth, care as well as discipline provided by its wardens - Mrs Shivali Narula the girls warden and Mr. Mahesh Parmar - the Boys warden. The ambience surrounding the hostel is one of pure youtfullness and unadulterated innocence, with students from all corners of India coming together to stay under one roof. It truly epitomises the home away from home, metaphor, in every sense. Only a DPS Vasant Kunj hosteller can tell how much this brick building, within the sprawling campus of DPS VK, means to him / her. The facilities available to the students are comprehensive, and every comfort and need of the children is taken care of whether it is the AC and watercooler in summer or water heaters during winters every aspect of comfort is catered to. There are grand dining halls for both boys and girls where vegetarian delicacies are served. The strategic location of the hostel near the Principal’s residence, with the wardens residing within the hostel further answers the security of the students. There is a recreation room with a T.V and a music system where the students can take respite after an exhausting day. DPS VK hostel is thus a haven of tranquility that stimulates the outstation student to achieve academic excellence with optimum focus.
